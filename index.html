What is Wozoelo?

Welcome to Wozoelo. Not quite a blog, not quite a magazine,Wozoelo is... a blog of blogs. You can search by topic or by your favorite author. We are a loose fitting group of humans looking for a way to share knowledge and experiences on a loose fitting conglomeration of subject matters. Unless clearly marked “fiction”, you can expect everything in Wozoelo to be the author’s honest understanding of a given topic. Read us, share us, join us, come for a visit, leave changed. Wozoelo is a magical internet space of education and exchange.
